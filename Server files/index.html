Founder CEO Message

As a locally owned Kenyan company we distinguish ourselves by providing world-class contract logistics - effectively promoting and delivering freight forwarding, handling and customs clearance services from service points in Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Zambia, South Sudan, Rwanda, Burundi, Somalia, Ethiopia and DRC through Air Freight, Sea Freight, Rail Freight, and Road Transport.

We come to work each day for one reason: to make things work for you – not merely on paper, not just conceptually, but practically, on the ground. Our strength resides in our expertise, track record, in-depth knowledge of our business and region and, most of all, in highly motivated and trained people. We are thus able to provide solutions to even the most difficult customs and logistics problems, ensuring that our customer’s cargo reaches its final destination on time.

We want to evolve and keep abreast of changes and development. We strive to be dynamic and adaptable, we don’t look passively on while the world passes by us. We operate under the motto “Evolving Solutions” confident that each of our employees act responsibly and make decisions that will benefit our customer.

This is our promise.




Contact us and let us show you how we can make your business and trade in
East and Central Africa experience cost-savings in the form of equity or time!
Mr. Shem Okottah – Founder CEO
