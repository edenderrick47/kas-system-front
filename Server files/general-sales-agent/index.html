General Sales Agent (GSA)


KAS Logistics Services market strategies to promote sales and to achieve targets set by the airlines. We promote sales through marketing and commercials or under the guidance of the airline.

General Sales Agents (GSA) play a vital role in the growth and development of the air cargo industry by being an important vertebra in the backbone of the air cargo supply chain. Gone are the days when GSA’s were appointed only as sales and marketing representatives who were responsible for selling air cargo space. KAS Logistics Services GSA conducts responsibly end-to-end business functions of the air cargo.

GSA Services

  • Cargo Space Sales
  • Office Space of International Standard
  • Efficient & Skilled Staff
  • Payment of Local & Other Taxes
